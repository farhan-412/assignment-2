Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Early childhood development, or ECD, is about the ‘whole child’ – the physical, social and emotional, cognitive thinking and language progression of each young individual.

Elementary School

A primary school, elementary school, or grade school is a school for primary education of children who are 4 to 10 years of age (and in many cases, 11 years of age). Primary schooling follows preschool and precedes secondary schooling.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school, also known as intermediate school, junior high school, junior secondary school, or lower secondary school, is an educational stage between primary school and secondary school.
